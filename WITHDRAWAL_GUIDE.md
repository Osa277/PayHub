# 💰 How to Withdraw Your Money from PayHub

## **Quick Summary**
Your deposited funds are in your PayHub wallet. Here are **3 ways** to withdraw them:

---

## **Method 1: Withdraw to Your Bank Account (Recommended)** ✅

### Step 1: Go to Wallet Page
1. Open your PayHub app
2. Navigate to **Wallet** page
3. You should see your balance

### Step 2: Add Your Bank Account
1. Scroll down to **"Bank Accounts"** section
2. Click **"Add Account"** button
3. Fill in:
   - **Account Name**: Your name as on bank account
   - **Account Number**: Your 10-digit account number
   - **Bank**: Select your bank from dropdown (9 Nigerian banks available)
4. Click **"Add Account"**

### Step 3: Initiate Withdrawal
1. After adding bank account, look for **"Withdraw"** button
2. Click it to open withdrawal modal
3. Select the bank account you just added
4. Enter amount to withdraw
5. Review the fee (1% withdrawal fee applies)
6. Click **"Withdraw"**
7. Money will be sent to your bank account via Paystack

**Time**: Usually arrives within **5-30 minutes**

---

## **Method 2: Transfer to Another PayHub User** 🔄

If you have a friend/colleague with PayHub:

1. Go to **Wallet** → **Transfer Money**
2. Enter recipient's email address
3. Enter amount
4. Click **"Send"**
5. They receive it in their PayHub wallet instantly

⚠️ **Note**: Both wallets must have the **same currency** (both USD or both NGN)

---

## **Method 3: Withdraw Directly from Paystack** 💸

If the in-app withdrawal doesn't work:

1. Go to **Paystack Dashboard**: https://dashboard.paystack.com
2. Login with your Paystack account
3. Go to **Balances** section
4. Click **"Withdraw"**
5. Add your bank account details
6. Enter amount
7. Click **"Withdraw"**

**This is the ultimate source** - your PayHub account is connected to your Paystack account.

---

## **Troubleshooting**

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Withdrawal button not showing | Make sure you have a bank account added first |
| "Insufficient balance" error | Check your wallet balance covers amount + 1% fee |
| Bank account won't save | Verify your account number is exactly 10 digits |
| Withdrawal stuck/failed | Go to Paystack dashboard to check status |
| Paystack account issue | Contact Paystack: support@paystack.com |
